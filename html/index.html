Opportunities

Are you ready to leap to the next level?

Who are we?

We are Alternative Beauty Services, the exclusive distributor in Ontario for Redken, Sexy Hair, Tressa, Spa Ritual, Artistic, and Mirabella products. Explosive growth since our start-up 9 years ago continues to generate exceptional opportunities for exceptional people. If you are one of those people, read on! We are currently looking for a District Sales Representative for the Windsor region. If you are a highly motivated person with an entrepreneurial attitude and competitive spirit, then we invite you to contact us.


Job responsibilities:

Working under the direction of a District Sales Manager, you will be responsible for:

  • Servicing an existing customer base on a weekly or bi-weekly basis
  • Developing new customer relations through on-going personal contact
  • Providing product information and training to customers as required
  • Working with customers to grow their businesses

While we are a distribution company, our philosophy goes beyond the typical distributor. Our motto is "Trust The Difference" and you immediately see that we are different. We want to build long term relations with our customers and that requires an exceptionally high level of customer service. Our reputation is unequalled. Our product lines are extensive. Our customer service is exceptional.

If you have a strong drive to provide an exceptional customer experience, a willingness to do what is necessary to keep our customers happy, and a desire to learn, then we want to talk with you. But, you should note – this is a sales position. While you will start with a group of current customers, you will excel if you are able to open new doors through prospecting, cold calling and general hard work. Your rewards will be directly related to your efforts!


Our ideal candidate:

  • At least 1 year sales experience, preferably in a related industry
  • Strong organization and time management capabilities
  • Excellent communication skills
  • The ability to multi-task and focus on many things at once
  • Living in the Windsor and surrounding areas

While Beauty Industry experience is an asset, it is not mandatory. We provide full on the job training, a pleasant and congenial place to work, and ample rewards for exceptional performance. This position is a combination of salary + bonus, with car, gas and cell phone allowance. Typically, new representatives are compensated in the $50K range, but top consultants in our firm generally earn in excess of $100K.

If you are interested, we invite you to submit your resume to: dsc@newellconsultants.com Alternative Beauty welcomes all candidates, but unfortunately, we will only be able to respond to you if you are selected for an interview, we will contact you.

No telephone calls, please and thank you :)
